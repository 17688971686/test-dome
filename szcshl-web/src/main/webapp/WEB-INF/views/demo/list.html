目前支持的Odata语法(后续会逐步更新)：

相等: http://localhost/Products?$filter=Category eq 'Toys'
不相等: http://localhost/Products?$filter=Category ne 'Toys'
小于： http://localhost/Products?$filter=Price lt 10
大于等于和小于等于： http://localhost/Products?$filter=Price ge 5 and Price le 15
包含： http://localhost/Products?$filter=substringof('zz',Name)
分页：http://localhost/Products?$skip=20&$top=10 （从20条数据开始，取10条）
orderby: http://localhost/Products?$orderby=Price
orderby desc: http://localhost/Products?$orderby=Price desc
